Paweł Waraksa
Product Designer

I’m a Designer based in Poland, specializing in Product Design, Research, and Interaction Design. In short, I help people build companies, develop brands and digital products with a focus on simplicity and scalability.

For the last couple of months I've been leading the design team at early-stage startup called Huspy, we just raised $4 million in seed round.

Previously, I worked at Revolut for two years, where I was a responsible for Core Products such as: Accounts, Transactions, Cards and Vaults. I helped scale the Product from 3 million to over 12 million users and worked with pretty much every team in the company, including Revolut for Business. Before I left, Revolut raised $500 million in Series D, which made it one of the most valuable fintech startups in the world.

In my spare time I enjoy learning new things, so I like reading books, listening to music & podcasts, and spending hours reading articles & finding new information about technology, psychology and science. I generally like to "know things".
